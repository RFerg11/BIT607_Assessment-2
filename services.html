Our Grooming Process

Clean and Prep

Your dog starts with a gentle brush-out and a relaxing bath using high-quality shampoos suited to their coat. We follow this with a comfortable blow-dry to prepare their coat for grooming.

Groom and Care

This includes a tidy or full haircut (based on your preference or breed standard), nail trimming, and ear cleaning. Each step is done with care to keep your dog calm, safe, and comfortable.

Fluff and Finish

We give your dog a final brush, add any requested finishing touches, and make sure they look and feel amazing. At pick-up, we share how the session went and offer tips to keep them looking snazzy between visits.

Our Terms and Conditions

1. Health and Safety

Please let us know about any medical conditions, allergies, or behavioural issues before your appointment. We may stop a groom if a dog becomes too stressed, unwell, or unsafe to handle.

2. Coat Condition and Matting

If your dog’s coat is matted, we will always prioritise their comfort and safety. Severely matted coats may require a short clip for your dog’s comfort. Extra fees may apply for mat removal.

3. Behaviour and Handling

Dogs must be able to be safely handled. If a dog becomes overly stressed or aggressive, we may stop the groom. Additional handling fees may apply.

4. Cancellations and No-Shows

Please give at least 24 hours’ notice to cancel or reschedule. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee.

5. Late Arrivals

If you arrive late, your appointment may be shortened or rescheduled to avoid delays for other clients. Full charges may still apply.

6. Fleas and Parasites

If fleas or ticks are found, a flea treatment will be added automatically. Severe infestations may require rescheduling.

7. Senior Dogs and Puppies

Extra care is taken with senior dogs and puppies, but sessions may be adjusted for their comfort and safety.

8. Pricing

Prices vary based on breed, coat condition, size, and behaviour. Final pricing will be confirmed at the appointment.

9. Photography

We may take before-and-after photos for social media or promotional use. Please let us know if you would prefer your dog’s images not be used.

10. Owner Responsibility

Please ensure your dog has had an opportunity to toilet before their groom and is collected promptly when notified they are ready.
